## Project Overview

GoTrustWDU (TUR) is a fixed-supply BEP-20 utility token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain.

- No mint function
- No burn mechanism
- No buy, sell, or transfer tax
- Fully verified smart contract
- Publicly auditable on-chain data
