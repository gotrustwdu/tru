About GoTrust WDU

GoTrust WDU (TUR) is a BEP-20 utility token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain with a permanently fixed total supply.

The project focuses on transparency, simplicity, and publicly verifiable smart contract behavior. All core information is openly documented and accessible.

GoTrust WDU was not created for fundraising, investment, or speculative purposes. There was no ICO, IEO, IDO, private sale, or public sale.

The term “Trust” refers solely to transparency, verifiability, and open documentation. It does not imply custodial services, fiduciary responsibility, or guarantees of any kind.

GoTrust WDU is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Binance, Trust Wallet, or any third-party organization.

The project is maintained by an identifiable founder with public professional profiles, ensuring accountability and compliance with transparency standards.
