Project Overview

gotrustwdu Token (TUR) is a fixed-supply BEP-20 token deployed on BNB Smart Chain. The project aims to provide a minimal, transparent token that projects and communities can use for simple utilities, rewards, and educational purposes. The contract is intentionally simple: no mint, no burn, no tax, and only the name/symbol metadata may be changed by the owner.

Objectives

  • Provide a trustworthy, verifiable token foundation.
  • Support community rewards, educational uses, and small dApp utilities.
  • Maintain full transparency and open-source code.

How TUR will be used

TUR is intended for off-chain integrations and lightweight on-chain utility where appropriate: community incentives, gated access, in-app credits for mini-games, and educational programs. TUR is not presented as an investment product.
